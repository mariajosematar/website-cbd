PROJECT

CANNABHANGA is a cannabis company based in Uruguay and Argentina, dedicated to organic and sustainable production of high quality cannabis and its derivatives, for global markets.

Mission

Provide organic and natural cannabis products to improve people's quality of life. Mission Provide organic and natural cannabis products to improve people's quality of life.

Vision

A fully integrated craft company using the best methods and values in each process.

COMPANY MAIN OBJECTIVES:

  • Greenhouse and outdoor cannabis flowers of different strains for retail and customers.

  • Blockchain traceability from seed to final product.

  • Seeds and clones commercialization for local & international producers, with strategic partnerships.

  • Oil extraction for therapeutic and medicinal products.

  • Biomass, grain and fiber large scale production for bioconstruction, textiles and other subproducts.

  • Research and development of own cannabis genetics and native microorganisms with local institutions.

  • Expand from South America to the rest of the world.

  • Cannabhanga brand for its own cannabis products.
