Sustainability, Quality and Corporate Social Responsibility:

We are a growing company in balance with nature.

We care about the earth and people.

That's why we develop our activities with the least environmental impact possible.

We believe in circular economies and organic behaviors.

Certifications:

We do all our processes under the guidelines of the World Health organization and the European Medicine Agency.

We obtained the GACP certification (Good Agricultural and Collecting practices of medicinal plants).

We are in the process of an organic international certification, as well as a B company certification.

Recycling

We recycle at Campo Limpio, a national program for the collection and final destination of Phytosanitary products and fertilizers containers, where they also receive and recycle plastics, glass and cardboard.

https://campolimpio.org.uy/

Education & Investigation

University of the Republic of Uruguay - Chemical Engineer Faculty (UdeLar):

Study program for Chemical Engineering career thesis.

Main aspects: Research and experiments of cannabidiol (CBD) ethanol extraction.

University of Buenos Aires, Argentina - Agronomy Faculty (UBA)

Members of the Cannabis Study Group G.E.T. of the University, composed of teachers, graduates and students.

Main aspects: Collaborative investigation of cannabis production in Uruguay and Argentina.

University of the Republic of Uruguay - Faculty of Biochemistry - Mycology (UdeLar):

Financed by Basic Sciences Development Program from Uruguay (PEDEClBA) through the National Council for Innovation, Science and Technology (CONICYT).

Main aspects: Study of biological control for plagues and diseases in cannabis plants.
